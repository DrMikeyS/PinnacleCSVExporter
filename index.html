Pinnacle CSV Exporter & Dashboard

Version 0.9.0.1 Beta

There are three parts to the system:

  1. Convert Pinnacle Data to CSV
  2. Merge Accubook Sessions in Single CSV
  3. Compare Pinnacle List to Accubook

The system also provides a very basic dashboard with some key data points you may need.

If you find this useful, checkout the COVID Vaccine QR Generator which may also be useful for your clinic. Also free!

How this Works & Data Security

All data is processed on your computer and at no point leaves your machine. The source code is open should you wish to inspect it.
